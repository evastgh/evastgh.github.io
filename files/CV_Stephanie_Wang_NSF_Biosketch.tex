\section{About}
\cvline{}{
    My research interests are in geometry processing and physics simulations. I love exploring mathematical solutions that improve existing algorithms and pipelines in computer graphics. 
}
\cvline{Geometric optimization}{
    Many geometry processing tasks boil down to optimization problems, many of which are nonconvex. New mathematical insights can help discover the convex equivalent problems which comply with the fast and robust convex solvers. 
}
\cvline{Physics modeling}{
    Physical phenomena like solids, fluids, friction, fracture are crucial in building a virtual world. Existing algorithms often have to choose between performance and accuracy. Mathematical theories can inspire novel discretization schemes that are both faster and better at conserving important physical quantities. 
}
